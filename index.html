About Me

A highly motivated and analytical professional shifting from a top-performing technical support role to a dedicated career in cybersecurity. My journey is built on a foundation of hands-on, self-taught technical skills and a passion for protecting systems.

The Foundation

My journey into technology began not in a classroom, but with a screwdriver and a desire to make my games run faster. I taught myself to build, troubleshoot, and optimize my own computers, a self-starter approach that defines my career.

From Hands-On Support to Systemic Insight

I honed my technical communication skills in high-pressure environments, from remote IT troubleshooting that saved thousands in service calls to mentoring over 100 students as a 3D modeling instructor. My most recent role at Foundever (Cash App), where I became a 3-time MVP, was my turning point. On the front lines of financial tech, I saw firsthand how systemic process flaws created vulnerabilities. This shifted my focus from fixing individual problems to wanting to secure the entire system, leading me directly to cybersecurity.

Core Philosophy

I operate on the belief that the strongest security posture is built on continuous learning and acknowledging that the human element is the most critical variable. My objective is to be a reliable and forward-thinking defender, leveraging technical acumen to mitigate operational risk.

Cybersecurity Projects

Home Lab & Threat Analysis

A virtual lab simulating a corporate network to practice threat detection and incident response using tools like Wazuh SIEM, pfSense, and Wireshark.

Security Scripting & Automation

Python scripts to automate security tasks, including a network port scanner and a log file anomaly detector.

Vulnerability Assessment

Identifying and reporting on system vulnerabilities using tools like Nmap and OpenVAS, followed by professional remediation reports.
